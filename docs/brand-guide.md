# Altvia Brand Guide

## Brand Positioning

**Tagline**

Map your movement. Understand your terrain.

**Core Idea**

Altvia is a geospatial intelligence platform that visualizes movement, performance, and terrain insights. It combines organic, landscape-driven visuals with precise, actionable data.

## Tone And Voice

- Analytical, not motivational
- Calm, confident, observational
- Clear, descriptive, data-first

**Example phrasing**

- Instead of "Great run!" use "Route efficiency improved across elevation segments."
- Instead of "Track your progress!" use "Path coverage analyzed for optimal traversal."

## Color System

### Primary Palette

- `Deep Terrain`: `#0F172A` for the dark, grounding base
- `Forest Core`: `#1B5E20` for nature and stability
- `Clay Ridge`: `#A47148` for warmth and earth
- `Sky Fade`: `#4FC3F7` for soft sky highlights

### Accent And Data Highlight

- `Electric Blue`: `#00BFFF` for routes, paths, and live activity overlays

### Supporting Colors

- `Mist Gray`: `#E5E7EB`
- `Stone`: `#6B7280`
- `Shadow`: `#0A0A0A`

### Data Viz Gradients

- Low: muted green
- Medium: warm clay
- High: deep brown and dark tones
- Active paths: electric blue

## Typography

### Headings

- `Sora` or `Inter Tight`
- Structured, modern, tech-forward

### Body

- `Inter`
- Readable and clean

### Data And Metrics

- `JetBrains Mono`
- Used for pace, elevation, splits, and route metrics

## Visual Language

**Core Concept**

Data revealed through terrain.

### Rules

1. Use diffusion and halftone imagery.
2. Images fade at the edges and become denser in focal areas.
3. Imagery should be generated, not illustrated or stock.
4. UI overlays should use semi-transparent surfaces with soft blur and glass treatment.

### UI Surfaces

- Dark base with soft elevation
- Slight transparency over imagery

### Borders

- Very subtle
- Preferred border tone: `#2A2A2A`

### Buttons

- Primary: forest green fill with a soft glow hover
- Secondary: outline, minimal treatment

### Iconography

- Thin line icons
- Slightly rounded shapes
- Inspired by maps, paths, and nodes

## Landing Page Structure

### Hero Section

- Full-screen terrain diffusion image
- Headline: "Understand your movement across terrain."

### Section Themes

1. Analyze: density and activity visuals
2. Plan: route and path visualization
3. Optimize: terrain and elevation insights

### Callouts

- Routes appear in electric blue for dynamic overlay highlights

## What To Avoid

- Bright neon colors outside electric blue
- Fitness cliches such as flames, emojis, or generic runner tropes
- Overly playful UI
- Stock photography

## Brand Snapshot

Altvia is a geospatial performance intelligence platform: terrain-focused, data-driven, premium in feel, and centered on dynamic route visualization in electric blue.

## Design Direction Notes

- The product should feel premium, restrained, and precise.
- Terrain should act as both a visual metaphor and a structural concept for the interface.
- Data overlays should feel discovered within the landscape, not pasted on top of it.
- The logged-in product should remain map-first.
